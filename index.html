Safety measures

When planning school trips, ensure all students are supervised by responsible adults with a clear student-to-adult ratio. Provide emergency contact information to staff and students, and carry a fully stocked first-aid kit for prompt response to any incidents.

Safety Tips

Safety Tips for Protecting Students on a School Trip

Plan the route

Plan the route of the school trip carefully, considering factors such as traffic, road conditions, and potential hazards along the way.

Assign responsible adults

Ensure that there are enough responsible adults accompanying the students on the trip to provide proper supervision and support.

Emergency contact information

Provide emergency contact information to both staff and students, including phone numbers of parents or guardians, the school, and local emergency services.
